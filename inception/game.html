Controls

Outer game (BananaBread, a port of the Cube 2/Sauerbraten FPS)

  • WASD to move
  • Mouse to orient and shoot
  • Space to jump
  • e to toggle edit mode
  • ` open menu (add/remove bots, etc.)

Inner game (Boon = Doom code/PrBoom + Freedoom content)

  • Arrow keys to move
  • Control to shoot
  • Space to activate (aliased with outer game...)
  • | open menu (start game, change keybindings, etc.)
